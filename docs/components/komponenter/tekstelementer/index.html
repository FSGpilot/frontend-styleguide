Tekstelementer

Tekstelementer show tabular data in columns and rows.

Headings

All HTML headings, <h1> through <h6>, are available. .h1 through .h6 classes are also available, for when you want to match the font styling of a heading but still want your text to be displayed inline.

<div class="container">
    <div class="row">
        <div class="col-6">
            <h6>As heading tags</h6>
            <h1>Heading 1</h1>
            <h2>Heading 2</h2>
            <h3>Heading 3</h3>
            <h4>Heading 4</h4>
            <h5>Heading 5</h5>
            <h6>Heading 6</h6>
        </div>
        <div class="col-6">
            <h6>As classes</h6>
            <div class="h1">Heading 1</div>
            <div class="h2">Heading 2</div>
            <div class="h3">Heading 3</div>
            <div class="h4">Heading 4</div>
            <div class="h5">Heading 5</div>
            <div class="h6">Heading 6</div>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>

Body copy

The Frontend styleguide's global default font-size is 16px, with a line-height of 1.428. This is applied to the <body> and all paragraphs. In addition, <p> (paragraphs) receive a bottom margin of half their computed line-height (10px by default).

<div class="container">
    <div class="row">
        <div class="col-12">
            <p>This is a normal body text </p><br>
            <p class="bold">This is a bold body text</p><br>
            <em>This is an italic body text </em>
        </div>
    </div>
</div>
