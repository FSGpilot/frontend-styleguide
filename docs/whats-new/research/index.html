Research

The Frontend Styleguide is made by taking empirical knowledge collected from research by leading UX-experts and mixing it with the fundament of the U.S. Web Design Standards. This results in a styleguide that is solid for building governmental self-service solutions, and enables building solutions with the average user in mind.

The Frontend styleguide is not trying keep up with the newest trends. However the styleguide is made to be able to dynamically evolve.
