Eksempler

These templates for a two small selfservice solutions are built from some of the components of the Styleguide. Each of these pages is flexible; you can add or remove components or sub-components to suit your needs. These templates are great starting point for prototyping or for trying the Styleguide to see if they’re a good fit for you.
